About

About Dr. Vishram Sagar Pandey

Profile

Dr. Vishram Sagar Pandey is a highly accomplished Neurosurgeon with over 15 years of experience in Neuro and Spine Surgery. He is associated with three premier hospitals in Siliguri — Desun Hospital, Aastha Hospital, and Avalon Hospital — bringing world-class neurological care to the region.

Having worked as an expert across multiple cities in India, Dr. Pandey brings a wealth of diverse clinical experience and has handled numerous complex neurological cases throughout his career. His reputation is built on the twin pillars of accurate diagnosis and empathetic patient care.

He completed his advanced super-specialisation — MCh in Neurosurgery — from NIMHANS, Bangalore, one of India's most prestigious neurological institutes, equipping him with the finest training in the field.

Speciality Interests

Areas of Expertise

Dr. Pandey's speciality interests combine cutting-edge minimally invasive techniques with comprehensive brain and spine surgical expertise.

✦ Primary Speciality 🦴

Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery

Advanced keyhole surgical techniques for spinal conditions that minimise tissue damage, reduce recovery time, decrease post-operative pain, and ensure faster return to daily life for patients.

✦ Primary Speciality 🔭

Neuro Endoscopy

State-of-the-art endoscopic neurosurgery for brain conditions such as hydrocephalus, intraventricular tumours and skull base lesions — precise, safe and highly effective.

✦ Primary Speciality 🧠

Brain & Spine Surgery

Comprehensive surgical management of the full spectrum of brain and spinal disorders including tumours, vascular conditions, trauma, degenerative diseases and congenital abnormalities.

🩺

Head Injury & Trauma

Emergency and elective surgical management of traumatic brain injuries, skull fractures, extradural and subdural haematomas with skilled and rapid clinical intervention.

⚡

Epilepsy & Seizure Management

Expert evaluation and surgical treatment of drug-resistant epilepsy and complex seizure disorders, helping patients achieve freedom from debilitating episodes.

🔬

Neuro-Oncology Surgery

Surgical treatment of primary and metastatic brain and spinal tumours with the goal of maximum safe resection to preserve neurological function and improve quality of life.

Conditions Treated

Neurological Conditions

Dr. Pandey diagnoses and treats the full range of neurological and spinal disorders — from common conditions to the most complex surgical cases.

01

Alzheimer's Disease

Significant decline in memory, thinking, learning and organising skills, mostly seen in patients over 65 years of age.

02

Dementia

A group of diseases affecting thinking, memory, reasoning, personality, mood and behaviour — commonly seen after age 85.

03

Encephalitis

Life-threatening brain inflammation caused by viral infections, insect-borne infections or autoimmune reactions.

04

Head Injury & Trauma

Brain injuries sustained from accidents or trauma — potentially causing severe damage if not assessed and treated promptly.

05

Seizure Disorders

Uncontrolled firing of electrical signals by brain cells due to malfunction, affecting neighbouring brain cells and body parts.

06

Epilepsy

Chronic condition causing repeated seizures due to abnormal electrical signals — expertly managed to restore daily functioning.

07

Brain Tumours

Abnormal growths within or around the brain requiring precise surgical evaluation, diagnosis and intervention.

08

Spinal Disorders

Disc herniation, spinal stenosis, degenerative spine disease and spinal cord injuries causing pain and loss of function.

Diagnostics & Procedures

Advanced Diagnostic Services

Equipped with advanced diagnostic tools to ensure accurate diagnosis and comprehensive treatment planning for every patient.

⚡

EEG

Electroencephalography to detect irregular electrical activity in the brain and identify conditions such as epilepsy and complex seizure disorders with precision.

🩻

CT Scan

Detects bone and vascular irregularities, brain tumours, cysts, herniated discs, epilepsy, encephalitis, spinal stenosis and intracranial bleeding in stroke patients.

🔬

MRI

High-resolution imaging to examine the brain and spinal cord, detect structural abnormalities, tumours, abscesses and congenital conditions with exceptional clarity.

🧠

Neurosurgery

Comprehensive surgical diagnosis and treatment for the full range of conditions affecting the central nervous system — brain, spine and peripheral nerves.
